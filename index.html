Welcome to Copperhead Electric

Powering Owensboro, One Home at a Time

Founded in 2025, Copperhead Electric is proud to bring reliable, high-quality electrical services to the Owensboro, KY area. Though we’re a new name in town, our team of licensed electricians brings decades of combined experience to every job we take on. From routine residential wiring to full-scale commercial installations, we’re ready to light the way. At Copperhead Electric, we’re driven by a simple mission: to deliver safe, efficient, and honest electrical work that stands the test of time. Our team takes pride in being dependable professionals who show up on time, communicate clearly, and get the job done right — the first time. Whether you're upgrading your service panel, installing new lighting, or need emergency troubleshooting, our experienced electricians are here to help. We believe that great service starts with trust, and we’re building our reputation one satisfied customer at a time. Copperhead Electric — Fast. Friendly. Wired to Serve.
